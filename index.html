Welcome

With over 40 years of experience, ABC Upholstery specializes in custom and restoration work on chairs, couches, boat seats, outdoor furniture, and more. Founded in 1979, our small, family-run shop takes pride in old-school craftsmanship with modern comfort in mind. Whether it’s bringing a favorite piece back to life or creating something new, we’re here to help.
